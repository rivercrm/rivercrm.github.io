Intelligent Loyalty

While traditional loyalty programs tend to focus on retention, intelligent customer engagement looks at every behavior a customer can do that provides value to the organization. Any customer interaction, such as creating an account, purchasing across categories or higher margin products, writing reviews, making referrals to friends, increasing frequency or length of site visits, participating in social sharing, sending an email instead of calling customer service, etc. can be measured, have a value assigned to it, and an associated incentive (of proportionate value) used to motivate behavior change.
